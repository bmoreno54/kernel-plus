# provenance

provenance is deterministic. who, when, from what. these are
structural facts, not narrated accounts.

a node's provenance is not a story about how it got here. it is
the traceable chain of what directed it into existence. the chain
is made of receipts — timestamps, identifiers, human actions —
not summaries.

provenance does not require a narrator. if an LLM participates
in producing content, that participation is a fact in the chain,
not the voice telling the chain. the record is structural. the
recorder is irrelevant.

provenance is fractal. a node has provenance. a relation has
provenance. an event that changes a node has provenance. the
granularity is not prescribed — it emerges from what the system
actually needs to trace.

the validation test for provenance: can you follow the chain
backward from any node to its origin using only structural
artifacts (IDs, timestamps, hashes, human confirmations)?
if you need someone to explain what happened, the provenance
is incomplete.

## vocabulary migration — the diachronic face of provenance

provenance applies to vocabulary itself, not just to artifacts.
every conceptual vocabulary begins in a domain-specific register
and migrates toward domain-independence through use. the migration
follows three stages:

1. **embodied encounter** — the insight exists only as a practice.
   no vocabulary yet. the commitment is tacit, expressed through
   action rather than language.
2. **metaphorical articulation** — the insight crystallizes in
   domain-specific metaphor. the metaphor carries the full
   commitment but requires initiation to decode.
3. **structural description** — the insight sheds its domain
   register and becomes portable. anyone can understand it
   without knowing the originating domain.

the metaphorical stage is load-bearing. it carries embodied
context that the structural description strips away. but the
structural description is portable. the migration is not loss —
it is compression. the metaphor is the FADE; the structural
description is the kernel+ atom.

this is the diachronic form of the veil of ignorance: not just
"does this insight pass the test?" but "how does a domain-specific
insight travel toward passing?" the answer: by abstracting FROM
concrete encounter, not by starting abstract.

provenance makes this visible when the earlier registers are
preserved as archaeological artifacts rather than overwritten.
the chain from embodied practice through metaphor to structural
description IS the provenance of the concept. any collaborative
system that evolves its conceptual vocabulary over time will
exhibit this pattern.
