# node schema

this is the molecular composition of three foundational positions:
[node](node.md), [relation](relation.md), [provenance](provenance.md).

it does not add to them. it describes what emerges when they compose.

## composition, not inheritance

a node that has relations and provenance is not a subtype of "node."
it is node + relation + provenance composed at the boundary. the node
remains sovereign. the relations remain intersubjective. the provenance
remains deterministic. composition does not merge them — it holds them
in structural proximity.

this is molecular, not hierarchical. a molecule is not a bigger atom.
it is atoms in relation, where the relation itself is what creates
the new capacity. remove an atom and the molecule changes — but the
atom you removed is still whole.

## what composition produces

when node.md, relation.md, and provenance.md compose, three things
emerge that none of them states alone:

**the sovereignty test.** a node can be read alone (node.md). its
relations work from both sides (relation.md). its origin is
structurally traceable (provenance.md). these three together
constitute sovereignty. any one alone is partial.

**the adapter boundary.** a node does not speak another system's
vocabulary (node.md). what crosses a relation boundary is the
interface (relation.md). the chain of what happened is structural
fact, not narrative (provenance.md). these three together constitute
the airlock: translation at the boundary, not inside the node.

**holographic emanation.** every concrete node in the graph — lode.md,
checkout.md, image-hosting.md — carries the full capacity of this
molecular composition at its own resolution. a product entity is
sovereign the way node.md says. its edges are intersubjective the
way relation.md says. its origin is traceable the way provenance.md
says. it does not inherit from this schema. it emanates the same
pattern at the scale of what it actually is.

## what this node is not

this is not a template. nodes do not instantiate node_schema.md.
they compose the same atoms in their own way, discovering their
own shape (as node.md says: "each node discovers its own shape
by being what it is").

this is not a type system. the types that exist in the graph —
problem-node, product-entity, system-node — are names the graph
gave itself after the fact. they are documented in [schema.md](schema.md)
as emergent vocabulary, not prescribed here as categories.

this is not complete. the molecular cluster can accept new atoms.
if a fourth foundational position emerges (say, "surface" or
"encounter"), it composes into the molecule the same way: by
being held in structural proximity, not by being absorbed.

## deferral

everything beyond the three foundational positions and their
composition is deferred. formatting conventions, header schemas,
graph traversal mechanics, validation tooling — these are real
needs that will create pressure when they're needed. that
pressure is information. premature structure is not.
