## what this node is

the zero-point of the ecosystem. every other node exists in relation
to this one. modeling this explicitly means the ecosystem can reason
about what you need, what you've already done, and what you might
want to do next — without re-explaining from scratch each session.

## what this node is not

not surveillance. it does not track behavior, monitor productivity,
or optimize for engagement. it models relationships so agents can be
helpful collaborators rather than blank slates. you control what
accretes here. anything that feels wrong gets pruned.
