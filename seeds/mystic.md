## The Immanate

Every living system requires permanent exteriority — material it
cannot metabolize. The mystic domain IS the ecology's encounter
with its own outside. What lives here should resist full
systematization. If you can fully FADE it, it probably belongs
in research/ instead.

That said: FADE still applies. You can Find, Analyze, and Decompose
a practice or text. The Exploit phase is where mystic work diverges
— exploitation here often means deepened practice rather than
extracted utility.

## Sovereignty Note

Spiritual traditions are especially vulnerable to capture by
institutional authority. The same sovereignty principles apply:
the practice serves you (the practitioner), not the institution.
If a tradition requires suppressing your feedback system to
participate, apply the bracket test (see harvests, Atom 21).
