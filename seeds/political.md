## Capture Diagnostics

Political engagement is especially vulnerable to capture:

- [ ] Is the platform you're organizing on extracting more value
      from your engagement than the cause receives?
- [ ] Are you performing politics for an audience or doing politics
      for the affected?
- [ ] Does your engagement require suppressing your own alignment
      signals? (If yes: you may be captured.)
- [ ] Is the institution you're working with responsive to the
      constituency it claims to serve?
