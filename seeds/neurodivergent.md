## What This Document Is

A self-observation surface for how your particular nervous system
interacts with your ecology. Not a diagnosis. Not a deficit model.
A structural description of how you actually work — including the
parts that standard tooling and standard advice don't account for.

The ecology adapts to the human, not the human to the ecology.
This document tells the ecology (and its agents) how to adapt to
you specifically.

## The Somatic Veto

Your body has 0-class legislative power. If something feels wrong
— nausea, resistance, heaviness, avoidance — that's not laziness
or failure. It's information. The ecology should respect somatic
signals as architectural, not merely emotional.

Document known somatic signals and what they mean:

## Compensation vs. Sovereignty

Many neurodivergent people develop compensatory strategies that
mask rather than serve. The ecology should support YOUR operations,
not your performance of neurotypical operations:

      looks normal to others?
