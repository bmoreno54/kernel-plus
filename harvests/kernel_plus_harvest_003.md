## Integrated: Trunk Promotion

### Thermal Rendering Contract → `trunk/thermal_rendering.md` (NEW)

**Source:** Five-fold convergence documented by mapper run 9 (`shadow_mapper/analyses/thermal-rendering-contract-kernel-plus.md`), hearth FADE note (`hearth/notes/fade_thermal_classification_convergence_2026-04-24.md`), hearth signal (`hearth/signals/shadow_mapper_thermal_contract_kernel_plus_2026-04-25.md`)

**Domain-independent form:** Any system that renders heterogeneous items at variable resolution under finite attention decomposes rendering into four independent axes — categorical identity, temporal intensity, focal relevance (DOI), proxemic distance (zone) — and composes them into a contract that derives at encounter time.

**Evidence:** Five independent implementations (OMA calendar, omnibox broker, omnibox tree panel, omnibus.py, infinity engine) computed the same decomposition without cross-reference.

**Veil test:** PASS. A music recommendation engine, a code editor, a news feed, a filing system — anything rendering many items under finite attention — faces this same decomposition.

### Situated Derivation → thickened `trunk/cold_computation.md`

**Source:** Mapper run 8 condensation analysis (`shadow_mapper/analyses/condensation-event-cold-occlusion-fountain.md`)

**Domain-independent form:** Systems that derive state at encounter time from the observer's position, rather than maintaining a warm representation, compose across state management (cold computation), perception (occlusion rendering), and production (fountain principle) registers simultaneously. **Integration:** Added "Situated Derivation" section to cold_computation.md, linking the three registers and explaining why they compose.

## Harvest-Grade Atoms (Pass Veil, Not Yet Positional)

### Unconditional Capture Before Resolution

**Source:** `omnibox/notes/pidgin_capture_surface_from_hearth.md`

**Domain-independent form:** Input should be preserved unconditionally before resolution is attempted. Storage precedes interpretation. The captured atom accretes structure through co-occurrence over time rather than requiring structure at capture time. From outside the surface: scattered graffiti. From inside: toroidal — every mark reachable from every position through the tag substrate.

**Veil test:** PASS. Any system that receives unstructured input faces the choice: classify-then-store or store-then-classify. The second preserves the atom's sovereignty and enables emergent organization. Event sourcing, append-only logs, and zettelkasten all instantiate this.

### Unresolved Intent as Generative Signal

**Source:** `omnibox/notes/pidgin_fade_seeds_from_hearth.md`

**Domain-independent form:** When a system encounters unresolved intent — desire meeting absence — the absence of resolution is a generative signal, not a failure. The system's response should scale with the requester's engagement energy: abandon → tally, linger → analyze, push → full decomposition. Engagement energy is the signal that determines how much computation the unresolved intent warrants.

**Veil test:** PASS. Customer support escalation, search engine refinement, creative block → breakthrough — the pattern of unresolved intent scaling response with engagement energy is domain-independent.
