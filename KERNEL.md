## Kernel+ Architecture

This repo self-maintains through a stigmergic packaging loop.

### The `#kernel+` Tag
During FADE work anywhere in the ecology, atoms that appear domain-independent self-identify with `#kernel+` in place. They don't move — they mark themselves where they were discovered.

### The Tunable Veil
The veil of ignorance is the packaging parameter. At maximum opacity (kernel+0), only domain-independent structure passes. When packaging for a specific audience, declare target dimensions and the veil relaxes — producing kernel+music, kernel+gamedev, kernel+whatever. Same procedure, different attenuation.

### The Packaging Loop
1. **Harvest**: Walk the ecology, collect `#kernel+` tagged atoms.
2. **Reflect**: Apply the veil test. Does it survive stripping? Would it help a different human?
3. **Integrate**: Absorb into kernel/ or trunk/, or catalog in a harvest document.
4. **Version**: `git commit -F .git/COMMIT_MSG` (file-based messages for cross-platform compatibility).
5. **Push**: `git push origin main`

### Kernel+Extensions Economy
Bespoke packages assembled by tuning the veil. Each is forking-ready because the packaging process guarantees structural soundness. The kernel core is invariant; extensions are intentionally included domain material.
